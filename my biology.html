Biology

Definition of biology

Biology is derived from the two Greeck words BIOS which means life and LOGOS wich means study.

Branches of biology

The two main branches of biology are:

Zoology: zoology is defined as the studie of animals e.g dogs,cats,lames and even roosters.

Botany: Botany is defined as the study of plants e.g the flytrap plant.

Other branches of biology are:

Ecolpogy: Ecology involves the study of plants and animals (living thing) in relation to their enviroment.

Morphology: morphology involes the study of external featuers of plants and animals.

Anatomy:Anatomy is another branch of of biology which involves the study of internal structures of plants and animals.

Genetics: Genetics is the study of hereditry and variation of living things.

Physiology: Physiology is the study of how plants and animals function.
